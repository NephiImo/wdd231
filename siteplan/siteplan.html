Te's Krumbbs - Site Plan

Site Name

Name: Te's Krumbbs

Reason: This name captures the playful and nostalgic vibe of the brand while giving a direct hint at baked goods like banana bread. It's quirky and memorable.

Example domain (optional): teskrumbbs.com or krumbbsbakes.com

Site Purpose

The site will showcase Te's Krumbbs' banana bread and related baked goods. It allows customers to browse products, order online via WhatsApp or checkout, follow the brand on social media, and get a feel for the vibe and values of the bakehouse.

Scenarios

  • How can I place an order for banana bread online or through WhatApp?
  • What flavors and sizes are available, and how much do they cost?
  • Where can I find reviews or testimonials from past customers?

Color Schema

  • Warm Beige (#fffaf3): Used for the site background to give a clean, artisanal, home-baked feel.
  • Cocoa Brown (#3d2b1f): Used for main text and logo to reflect richness and warmth,like banana bread crust.
  • Caramel (#9b6a3f): Used for headings and buttons for contrast and a sweet, inviting touch.

Typography

  • Headings: Poppins (modern, friendly).
  • Body Text: Georgia (classic, readable)
